FlightStatus

This app has been my most ambitious project yet, as I set out to build the perfect travel companion app. Early on I put a focus on localization and accessibility to open the app to a wider audience. As the app took shape I was finally able to test it on a couple of flights I took in early June. After which I spend some more time adjusting things and after started a public beta for which about 50 people signed up. With their help I was able to fix a lot of little things and am very proud to have the app in store now.
